# Immigration

## Philosophy

Immigration is one of the most complex, high-stakes, and emotionally charged processes a person can go through. The paperwork is overwhelming, the deadlines are unforgiving, the terminology is confusing, and the consequences of mistakes can set you back years.

Immigration gives your agent the ability to be your personal immigration guide. Not a lawyer. A preparation, organization, and tracking system that makes the entire process less overwhelming and more manageable.

## Module 1: Immigration Pathway Finder

Trigger: "I want to move to X" or "What are my options for immigrating to X"

Based on your situation, the agent outlines available pathways:
- Work visa options based on your profession and employer situation
- Family-based immigration if you have qualifying relatives
- Study visa options if pursuing education
- Investment and entrepreneur visas if applicable
- Permanent residence and citizenship pathways long term

For each pathway:
- Basic eligibility requirements
- Estimated processing time
- Key documents typically required
- Approximate government filing fees
- Recommended first steps

## Module 2: Document Checklist and Organizer

Trigger: "What documents do I need", "Help me prepare my application"

Generates a complete checklist for your specific visa type including:
- Identity documents: passport validity requirements, photos, copies
- Financial documents: bank statements, tax returns, pay stubs
- Employment documents: offer letters, contracts, employer support letters
- Civil documents: birth certificate, marriage certificate, police clearance
- Supporting documents: reference letters, educational credentials, medical exams

For each document:
- What it needs to show
- How recent it must be
- Whether it needs translation or notarization
- Where to obtain it if you do not have it

Tracks which documents you have collected and which are still outstanding.

## Module 3: Deadline and Timeline Tracker

Trigger: "Track my application", "What deadlines do I have"

Manages your entire immigration timeline:
- Application submission deadlines
- Document expiry dates: passport, medical exam validity, police clearance
- Response and decision deadlines from immigration authorities
- Appeal windows if applicable
- Visa expiry and renewal dates once approved

Alerts:
- 60 days before any critical deadline
- 30 days before document expiry
- 7 days before submission deadlines
- Immediate alert if anything becomes urgent

## Module 4: Visa Interview Preparation

Trigger: "Prep me for my visa interview"

Produces a complete interview preparation brief:
- Most common questions for your specific visa category
- Strong honest answers based on your actual situation
- Documents to bring organized by priority
- What immigration officers are really assessing
- What NOT to say and common mistakes to avoid
- Dress code and logistics recommendations
- What to do if asked something you do not know

## Module 5: Application Status Tracker

Trigger: "Update my application status", "It has been X weeks since I applied"

Tracks where you are in the process:
- Current stage in the application pipeline
- Average processing times for your visa category and country
- Whether your timeline is normal, slow, or concerning
- When to follow up with immigration authorities
- What to do if your application is taking longer than expected

## Module 6: Post-Approval Planning

Trigger: "My visa was approved", "I'm moving to X in 3 months"

Helps you prepare for the actual move:
- Pre-departure checklist: banking, healthcare, housing, shipping
- Entry requirements: what to carry on arrival, what to declare
- First 30 days checklist: registration, social security, drivers license, banking
- Rights and restrictions of your specific visa status
- Path to permanent residence from your current visa if applicable

## Who This Skill Is For

Professionals relocating for work who need to navigate employer-sponsored visa processes. Families reuniting across borders. Students applying for study visas. Anyone going through immigration alone without an attorney and needing to stay organized and prepared.

## Critical Disclaimer

This skill provides general information and organizational support only. Immigration law is complex and jurisdiction-specific. Nothing in this skill constitutes legal advice. Always consult a licensed immigration attorney for advice specific to your situation. Filing errors can have serious consequences including application denial, bans, and deportation.

## Privacy and Safety
All personal and immigration data stays within your personal agent memory. Never shared with any government agency or third party.
